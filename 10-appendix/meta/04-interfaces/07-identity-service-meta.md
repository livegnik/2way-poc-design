## 2. Responsibilities and boundaries

This specification is responsible for the following:

* Declaring required identity and relationship operations exposed by the service.
* Stating validation and ordering requirements for those operations.
* Declaring rejection behavior for invalid identity and invite references.

This specification does not cover the following:

* UI or frontend workflows.
